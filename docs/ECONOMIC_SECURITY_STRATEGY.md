## Executive Summary

The National Security Strategy mandates that "the Intelligence Community will monitor key supply chains and technological advances around the world to ensure we understand and mitigate vulnerabilities and threats to American security and prosperity."

**GH Systems ABC provides the real-time, cryptographically verifiable intelligence compilation layer for supply chain security**—enabling the Intelligence Community to monitor, assess, and respond to supply chain threats in <500ms instead of weeks.

**Key Value Propositions:**
- **Real-time Supply Chain Monitoring**: <500ms threat intelligence compilation
- **Cryptographic Verification**: SHA-256 hash ensures intelligence integrity
- **Critical Mineral Tracking**: Monitor supply chains for rare earths, semiconductors, energy
- **Adversarial Influence Detection**: Identify foreign control of strategic assets
- **Supply Chain Risk Assessment**: Automated risk scoring for critical dependencies
