# Why ABC?

**ABC solves the verification problem for government AI intelligence**

---

## The Problem

**AI systems disagree, and there's no way to verify data quality.**

Government intelligence agencies deploy sophisticated AI systems to analyze threats. When multiple agencies analyze the same intelligence compilation and get different results, there's no objective way to verify:

- Did they analyze the same data?
- Is the disagreement due to data quality or methodology differences?
- Which assessment is more reliable?

**The result:** Weeks of manual conflict resolution, slower decision-making, and uncertainty in critical situations. For Foundry customers, these conflicts often lead to accusations of data quality issues—damaging reputation and consuming 40+ support hours per incident.

### Real-World Example

```
Palantir Foundry compiles intelligence → CIA AI: 85% confidence
                                      → DHS AI: 60% confidence
                                      → Treasury AI: 72% confidence

Question: Did they analyze the same data? No way to verify.

Result: 14 days to manually reconcile conflicts.
```

---

## The Solution

**ABC provides cryptographic proof that all agencies analyzed the same source data.**

ABC acts as the verification layer for Palantir Foundry—similar to how Chainlink provides data verification for Ethereum. When agencies disagree, ABC provides mathematical proof that the disagreement is methodology, not data quality.

**ABC makes Foundry unstoppable:**
- When agencies blame Foundry for conflicting results, ABC proves Foundry delivered correct data
- Agencies can trust their differences are analytical, not data-related
- Conflict resolution becomes faster and more transparent
