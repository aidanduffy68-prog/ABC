# Manifesto Improvements Summary

### 2. **Enhanced Tone Alignment**

**Palantir Elements:**
- "Doctrine of sovereignty" (strategic framing)
- Historical precedent emphasis (Ford, Carnegie, Johnson)
- "National framework for power" (defense/intel focus)

**Twenty Elements:**
- "Operational security" (cybersecurity urgency)
- "Proactive deterrence" (vs. reactive defense)
- Accountability framing ("we know who to hold accountable")

**FinCEN Elements:**
- "Enforcing compliance" (regulatory focus)
- "Securing the financial frontier" (enforcement language)
- "Sovereign infrastructure" (jurisdiction and governance)
